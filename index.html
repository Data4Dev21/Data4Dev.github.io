Project #1

a dive into the Covid_19 Metrics
(Africa In Context)

The year 2020 saw the whole world hit by the covid_19 pandemic. The numbers emanating from data gathered seem to tell a positive story in terms of the continent Africa.
Could that be a result of comparison with other continents? This project sought to consider the metrics from Africa with Africa in context.
Data exploration was done using SSMS to do some key extractions which were further visualised in tableau desktop to give a graphical impression and also point out trends and insights.
